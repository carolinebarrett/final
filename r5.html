Recipe 5: Banana Bread!

I choose to make banana bread for my last cooking experience because even though this is technically baking this is one of my favorite things to make.

My Rating of this Recipe: 9/10

I have made this a decent amount of times before so it was very easy to make.

Listed below are the ingredients and instructions I followed to make this recipe at Simply Recipes.

Ingredients Necessary (Makes 2 Loafs):

  • 5-6 bananas
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • Dash of brown sugar and cinnamon
  • Optional: Chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 325Â°F.
  2. Mix everything in one bowl (you can you seperate for wet and dry ingredients but I used one bowl). If using frozen bananas make sure you take them out and they have some time to defrost.
  3. I did use a separate bowl for mashing up the bananas then added this to the bowl that has everything in it.
  4. Use a mixer to get all the lumps out, and spray your pans before putting the batter in them.
  5. Add brown sugar and cinnamon to the top layer of the pan and place in the oven.
  6. Cook for 45 minutes then I placed tin foil over them for the last 10 minutes (optional to prevent edges from burning). Cook for 55-60 minutes.
  7. Serve and Enjoy!

Personal Insights:

I usually follow this recipe when I make banana bread and I like baking better than cooking so I found making this recipe to be the most enjoyable to make.
